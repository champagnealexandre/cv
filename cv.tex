\section{Research Profile}
NASA Postdoctoral Fellow and physicist investigating the physical principles underlying the origin of life. 
My research integrates statistical mechanics, information theory, and evolutionary dynamics to understand how spatial environments drive the emergence of complexity. 
Currently developing quantitative frameworks for agnostic life detection and biosignature assessment.
